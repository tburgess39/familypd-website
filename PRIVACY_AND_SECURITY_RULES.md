# FamilyPD Privacy, Security, and Data-Minimization Rules

## Core rule

When FamilyPD can provide a feature without collecting personal data, it will not collect personal data.

If a proposed feature requires exact sensitive information, do not include the feature in that form. Redesign it around:

- general categories;
- optional non-sensitive labels;
- status or completion checkboxes;
- ranges instead of exact amounts;
- percentages;
- user-created nicknames;
- non-sensitive summaries;
- information stored outside FamilyPD.

## Information FamilyPD must not request

FamilyPD must not request or store:

- Social Security numbers;
- tax identification numbers;
- driver's license, passport, immigration, or government identification numbers;
- bank, routing, credit-card, debit-card, or financial-account numbers;
- passwords;
- authentication codes;
- security questions;
- exact home addresses;
- exact birth dates;
- medical diagnoses, medication lists, insurance numbers, or medical records;
- confidential school records or student identification numbers;
- alarm codes, gate codes, or detailed security-system information;
- detailed legal case information;
- documents commonly used for identity theft.

## Household members

Use general labels such as:

- Household Lead
- Co-Lead
- Family Member
- Adult Member
- Young Adult Member
- Teen Member
- Child Member
- Older Adult Member

Do not require legal names, exact ages, exact birth dates, email addresses, phone numbers, school names, or employer names.

## Finances

Use:

- general goals;
- spending categories;
- optional ranges;
- percentages;
- yes/no checkpoints;
- progress status.

Do not request exact income, account balances, debt balances, bank names, account numbers, card numbers, pay stubs, tax returns, or credit reports.

## Health

Use:

- general wellness goals;
- sleep, movement, hydration, stress, and appointment-completion reminders;
- non-diagnostic self-reflection;
- support-needed status.

Do not request diagnoses, medications, provider names, insurance information, treatment records, or medical documents.

## Education

Use:

- general learning goals;
- skill and credential interests;
- completion status;
- non-sensitive progress descriptions.

Do not request student identifiers, portal credentials, official transcripts, confidential discipline files, individualized education records, or exact school records.

## Safety

Use:

- general emergency procedures;
- drill completion;
- role assignments;
- preparedness checklists;
- reminders to store emergency contacts somewhere secure outside FamilyPD.

Do not request exact addresses, phone lists, alarm codes, gate codes, travel routines, medical details, or security-system information.

## Community data

Allow searches by general geography such as city, county, district, school, or ZIP code. Do not require or save a precise home address.

## Files

Before an upload, display a warning and require the user to confirm that the file does not contain sensitive information.

Apply a size limit and restrict accepted file types.

Do not promise that an automated scan can guarantee a file is safe.

## Exports and PDFs

Export only records intentionally marked for sharing.

Use generic filenames.

Do not place private reflections, restricted goals, or sensitive records in update packs.

Generated documents must remind users not to add confidential information.

## Examples

Never use the developer's name or the names of any family members in:

- examples;
- screenshots;
- sample records;
- demonstrations;
- test data;
- templates;
- generated documents.

Use generic household and member labels.


## Meeting-planner privacy

Use general labels for facilitators, participants, and responsible roles.

Do not store:

- private meeting links or access codes;
- exact street addresses;
- phone numbers or email addresses;
- diagnoses or medical records;
- confidential school records;
- financial account details or exact balances;
- passwords, IDs, or legal documents.

Private Family Member preparation remains in the member workspace and is not included in Household Update Packs.
