What happens when you open the OS

Sign in, review permissions, and enter your workspace.

  1. 1

    Open app.familypd.org

    The app may ask you to sign in with Google so it can recognize your account.

  2. 2

    Review the Google permission screen

    Google shows the access the app is requesting. Read the screen before approving.

  3. 3

    Return to FamilyPD OS

    After approval, the app opens your household workspace and the tools connected to your account.

Why permission is needed

FamilyPD OS uses sign-in to authenticate the person opening the workspace. When a feature creates or manages a FamilyPD document in Google Drive, Drive access is needed for that user-requested action.

  • Authentication identifies the signed-in user.
  • Drive permission supports FamilyPD-created folders and documents when the user chooses those features.
  • The app does not need permission to read or send Gmail.

How FamilyPD uses data

FamilyPD does not use household information for advertising, data sales, or unrelated profiling. Account data is used for authentication and to provide the workspace functions the user requests.

Your information stays under your control.Use the same care you would use with any private family workspace: share only what is needed, review access, and avoid storing passwords or information that does not belong in the app.
