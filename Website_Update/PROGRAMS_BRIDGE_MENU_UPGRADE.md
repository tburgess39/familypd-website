## New Family–School–Community Bridge Center

New page:

- `programs/bridge-center/index.html`

New supporting files:

- `assets/data/family-school-community-bridge.json`
- `assets/js/family-school-community-bridge.js`

The public planner supports three viewpoints:

- Family or caregiver
- School or educator
- Community organization

It covers:

- Health, Safety & Well-Being
- Relationships, Communication & Support
- Education, Digital Skills & Career Pathways
- Finances, Benefits & Household Stability
- Goals, Opportunity & Family Progress

Each connection plan includes:

- signs that the area may need attention;
- household starting actions;
- possible school contacts;
- possible community contacts;
- questions to ask;
- role-specific recommendations;
- household project ideas;
- official learning-resource suggestions.

## Privacy and app boundary

The Bridge Center is a public practice and education tool.

It does not:
- collect family referrals;
- determine eligibility;
- save family case records;
- provide professional medical, legal, financial, or emergency advice;
- request private student or household information.

Real implementation and saved records belong in the FamilyPD App at `app.familypd.org`.
