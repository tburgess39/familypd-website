Interactive project generator

Turn a FamilyPD lesson into something young people can build.

Choose an age group, pillar, project type, and time. The lab creates a challenge, steps, test questions, and a printable project plan.

Choose language

Project directions switch languages. Some skill labels remain in English while the full bilingual library is expanded.

Step 1

Choose the kind of challenge.

Step 2

Review the creator challenge.

Your creator challenge will appear here. Choose your options and select Generate Idea.

Step 3

Make the project your own.

Creator promise

I will build something useful, test it, protect private information, give credit to sources, and ask for help when safety or accuracy matters.

This lab does not

  • collect or publish children’s projects
  • ask for a child’s full name, school, address, or account
  • connect to Google Drive or GitHub
  • replace adult supervision or professional safety guidance

Project cycle

  1. Choose a real family need.
  2. Build a small first version.
  3. Test it with family.
  4. Improve what is unclear.
  5. Share only after adult review.
