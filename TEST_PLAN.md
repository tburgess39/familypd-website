# FamilyPD v6.0.0 Live Test Plan

## English and Spanish

1. Start in English.
2. Open Meetings.
3. Confirm all meeting headings, buttons, field guidance, topic tools, Help content, and meeting cards are English.
4. Switch to Español.
5. Confirm the complete meeting frame changes to Spanish.
6. Switch back to English.
7. Repeat the language cycle three times.
8. Confirm saved household wording does not get automatically translated or replaced.

## Household Lead meeting planning

1. Create a Draft meeting.
2. Select a teen or young-adult role as facilitator.
3. Select several participant labels.
4. Add one topic from each type:
   - suggested FamilyPD topic;
   - custom topic;
   - recent-news topic.
5. Edit the title, purpose, questions, outcome, and planned time.
6. Add a previous-meeting recap.
7. Add materials and an optional meal plan.
8. Save as Scheduled.
9. Reopen and add notes, decisions, and action items.
10. Mark one action complete.
11. Save as Completed.
12. Duplicate the meeting.
13. Carry unfinished action items into the duplicate.
14. Archive a test meeting.

## News discussion

1. Search each FamilyPD pillar in English.
2. Open several returned articles.
3. Confirm each displayed result has a title, publisher/domain, date, and link.
4. Add a story to the agenda.
5. Confirm the article remains attached after saving and reopening.
6. Confirm the article link appears in the meeting card.
7. Test Spanish news search.
8. Confirm a service error shows a clear message instead of breaking the meeting planner.
9. Confirm no article older than seven years is accepted.

## Family Member workspace

1. Create a new Household Update Pack from the Household Lead workspace.
2. Import it into a Family Member workspace.
3. Confirm meetings appear read-only.
4. Confirm article links can be opened.
5. Open a meeting and save private preparation:
   - questions;
   - topic suggestion;
   - general participation support.
6. Confirm private preparation does not enter a new Household Update Pack.

## PDFs

1. Generate an Agenda PDF before the meeting.
2. Confirm meeting basics, participants, materials, meal, topics, prompts, article link, guidebook citation, and References appear.
3. Complete the meeting.
4. Generate a Minutes PDF.
5. Confirm notes, decisions, action items, guidebook reference, and selected article references appear.
6. Repeat PDF generation in Spanish.

## Privacy

Try entering:

- an email address;
- a phone number;
- an exact street address;
- a password label;
- an account-number label;
- a medical-record label;
- a government-identification label.

Confirm FamilyPD blocks the entry and requests a general description.

## Regression

Confirm Household Identity, roles, Goals & Checkpoints, bilingual switching, Update Packs, Help, PDF generation, repair, and Drive links still work.
