# Family PD Verified News Update Guide

The `assets/data/family-news.json` file controls the Family News & Prevention Discussion page.

## Story requirements

1. Use a recognized news organization or official public agency.
2. Confirm the publication date.
3. Use stories published within the last seven years.
4. Connect the story to at least one Family PD pillar.
5. Focus on practical prevention, preparation, family policy, skills, systems, or support.
6. Link directly to the original article.
7. Do not copy the full article. Write a short factual summary in your own words.
8. Add family discussion prompts and proactive actions.
9. Update the `last_verified` date every time the collection is reviewed.

The page automatically hides stories older than seven years.
