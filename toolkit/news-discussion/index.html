Suggested discussion process

Move from headline to proactive family action.

1

Read

Open the original article and separate verified facts from assumptions.

2

Connect

Identify which Family PD pillar and household risks are involved.

3

Discuss

Invite age-appropriate questions, feelings, concerns, and perspectives.

4

Prevent

Ask what policy, system, skill, or preparation could reduce the risk.

5

Act

Choose one realistic family action and assign responsibility.
