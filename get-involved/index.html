Nevada launch priorities

Areas where experience and support can make a difference.

These are developing needs—not promises of paid employment or permanent positions. Every role or partnership will be discussed and confirmed individually.

01

Program & Resource Review

Educators, counselors, family advocates, and subject-matter professionals who can review content for clarity and usefulness.

02

Technology & Digital Access

Website, application, accessibility, cybersecurity, audiovisual, and digital-skills support.

03

Community Outreach

Connections to families, schools, libraries, employers, neighborhood groups, and trusted local services.

04

Workshop & Event Support

Facilitation, setup, registration, family engagement, materials, childcare coordination, food, and logistics.

05

Funding & Sustainability

Grant research, sponsorship development, fundraising systems, budgeting, and resource planning.

06

Evaluation & Accountability

Feedback tools, outcome definitions, privacy-conscious data practices, program evaluation, and public reporting.

07

Language & Accessibility

Translation, plain-language review, disability access, culturally responsive feedback, and inclusive program design.

08

Professional Guidance

Appropriate legal, accounting, insurance, governance, nonprofit operations, and risk-management expertise.

Discuss Support

What happens next

A contribution begins with a conversation.

1

Introduce

Share the interest, need, resource, skill, or possible partnership.

2

Clarify

Discuss the audience, purpose, expectations, timing, capacity, and boundaries.

3

Confirm

Agree in writing on the role, scope, contact person, and next steps.

4

Contribute

Complete the agreed work while communicating changes or support needs.

5

Reflect

Review what worked, what was learned, and whether the partnership should continue.
