The Family PD Framework

Five pillars that work together.

Begin with health and safety, strengthen relationships, keep learning, build financial stability, and use goals to create direction.

Why the order matters

You can work on more than one pillar at a time, but a goal is harder to sustain when health is failing, relationships are unsafe, skills are missing, or finances are in crisis. The sequence helps families protect the foundation while still taking practical steps.

Pillar One

Health

Protect the foundation: physical, mental, emotional, spiritual health, rest, and safety.
