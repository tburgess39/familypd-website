## What the Kids & Teens Hub adds

- Age zones for:
  - Little Learners, ages 4–7
  - Builders, ages 8–12
  - Teen Launch, ages 13–18
- App-based and project-based creator pathways
- Youth creator roles
- Safety, privacy, source, and adult-review boundaries
- Direct links to the interactive Youth Creator Lab

## What the Youth Creator Lab does

Families can choose:

- age group;
- FamilyPD pillar;
- project type;
- available time.

The lab generates a challenge with:

- a project mission;
- skills practiced;
- build steps;
- family test questions;
- a public-safe reminder.

Young creators can then:

- customize the title and purpose;
- identify their creator role;
- list materials and adult support;
- name a reviewer;
- choose a target date;
- save the plan in the browser;
- copy it;
- download it as a text file;
- print it.

## Privacy boundary

The Creator Lab does not:

- collect or publish a child’s project;
- ask for a child’s full name, school, address, or account;
- connect to Google Drive;
- connect to GitHub;
- upload files;
- replace adult supervision.
